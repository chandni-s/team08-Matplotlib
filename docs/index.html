Team agreement

To make sure that the development process goes smoothly, the five of us agreed and sign off on a set a rules we need to follow:

TEAM AGREEMENT

Tools Being Used

Team work is a great opportunity one could have however, if not managed properly it can become a disastrous experience. In Forkestra, all the team members have tremendous group work experiences both in the industry and in school. Through these experiences, we all agree on the fact that the key for success in a team environment project is communication.

In order to keep the team focused on our project and have a successful term overall, we have agreed to use various modern development and project management tools. This includes: GitHub to manage our project code, a Slack channel for project related communication, a Facebook group chat for emergency issues and Trello which would act as our scrum board to keep up with the progress of our work. Considering the fact that the team members are taking other courses as well, we have agreed to have two mandatory meetings each week; one on Thursdays where each team member will decide what they will work on over the weekend. We agree to have another team meeting on Mondays after meeting with TA where we will examine the feedback given by our TA and go over the work done over the weekend. The frequency of these meetings will escalate as we approach closer to a deliverable due date for the project.
